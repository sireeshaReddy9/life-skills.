# 2. Which area do you think you need to improve on? What are your ideas to make progress in that area?

 I need to improve in two key areas: **gathering requirements properly** and **over-communication**.

## 1. Gathering Requirements Properly

To improve in this area, I will:

- Start documenting **Minutes of Meeting (MOM)** during every requirements discussion.
- Ask clarifying questions immediately if something is unclear.
- Seek early feedback during development to ensure I am on the right track.

## 2. Over-Communication

- Proactively share updates about progress, blockers, or delays.
- Inform the team immediately if requirements change or timelines are affected.
