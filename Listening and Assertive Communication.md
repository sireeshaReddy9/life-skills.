## 1. What are the steps/strategies to do Active Listening? (Minimum 6 points)
* Show you are listening through body language.

* Wait until they finish before you respond.
  
* Ask questions.
  
* let them finish and respond.
  
* Focus on the speaker and the topic.
  
* Make Eye contact.
---
## 2. According to Fisher's model, what are the key points of Reflective Listening?
* Pay close attention to what the person is saying.
  
* Say what you heard to make sure you understand.
  
* Ask questions to ensure you understand the speaker's message correctly.
  
* Acknowledge the speaker's feelings and thoughts, showing that you hear and understand them.
---
## 3. What are the obstacles in your listening process?
* Distractions of Noise, phone notifications, and other thoughts.
  
* Planning what to say next instead of focusing on the speaker.
  
* Losing interest in the topic.If I already know about the topic.
---
## 4. What can you do to improve your listening?
* Put away our phones, negative thoughts, and focus.
  
* Focus on the speaker's words, not your thoughts.
  
* Make eye contact and ask questions.
  
* Listen without judging or assuming.
---
## 5. When do you switch to a passive communication style in your day-to-day life?
* When you want to avoid arguments or fights.
  
* When you’re too shy or uncomfortable to share your opinion.
  
* When I’m dealing with authority figures or new people.
---
## 6. When do you switch into Aggressive communication styles in your day-to-day life?
*  When you are angry.
  
*  When you feel that you have been treated unfairly and want to fight back.
  
*   In stressful situations, when I’m feeling pressured.
---
## 7. When do you switch into Passive Aggressive (sarcasm/gossiping/taunts/silent treatment and others) communication styles in your day-to-day life?
*  When I’m feeling undervalued, like my efforts or opinions aren’t being appreciated.
  
*  When I don't want to argue but still show you are upset.
  
*  When you avoid direct problems but try to control things in another way.
---
